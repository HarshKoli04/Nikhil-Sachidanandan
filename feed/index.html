Aditya G. Nair’s primary research interests are at the intersection of fluid mechanics, data science, and graph theory. Formally trained in computational fluid dynamics, he is interested in a wide variety of problems ranging from uncovering the network structure of high-dimensional dynamical systems to the active control of fluid flows.

Teaching: ME482/682 Aerodynamics (Fall 2020, 2021, 2022), ME480/680 Compressible Flow (Spring 2021, Spring 2023), ME 793 Computational Fluid Dynamics (Spring 2022)
